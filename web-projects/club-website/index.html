Who We Are

We are The University Gaming Club, a small group of like-minded individuals who share a passion for gaming. Whether you're a skilled developer, someone who's less experienced, or just someone who enjoys playing their favourite games, everyone is welcome to join.

We host weekly social events where you can form new relationships, learn, or just try something new.

We host our very own Gaming Club Game Jam once every two weeks, starting on Fridays and spanning 72 hours. It's completely free to enter!

Join Us

Gaming Club Game Jam

Every two weeks, our club hosts the University Gaming Club Jam (UGC Jam), where anyone, regardless of skill or previous acknowledgements, can make a game over a single weekend. It's entirely free to join, whether you are a member or not. Our goal is to bring together anyone and everyone in order to make a memorable event.

The UGC Jam is a 72-hour jam where both solo and group developers can work to submit a game based on the theme and limitations given to them.

Events and Activities

We offer a variety of exciting and interactive events to our members, including but not limited to the following:

  • In-house casual tournaments for competitive games
  • Competitive e-sports tournaments against other universities (for a prize pool)
  • Developer masterclasses and workshops with known devs
  • Game nights
  • Focus groups and development assistance
  • Special events during holday period and uni break
